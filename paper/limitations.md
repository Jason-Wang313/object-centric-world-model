# Limitations

The scenes are synthetic and small, even with the added stress and dense-object panels. The learned model is a CPU NumPy semi-learned artifact, not a modern large-scale object-centric model. Repair selectors use diagnostic signals exposed by the toy generator. The toy model-family proxy panel is diagnostic only and does not replace independent graph-physics, latent dynamics, or diffusion world-model benchmarks. There is no real-robot evidence and no broad benchmark suite.

The paper-readiness target is therefore controlled synthetic v1. Broader claims require external environments, stronger learned models, and independent benchmarks.
